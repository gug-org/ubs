Application Guide

Welcome to the Admissions Office. We are pleased to assist you in your journey toward theological training. Follow the steps below to submit your application via email.

01

Confirm Eligibility

Review NCHE requirements for your desired program:

  • Certificate: UCE with 3 passes.
  • Diploma: UACE with 1 Principal Pass.
  • Bachelor's: UACE with 2 Principal Passes.
02

Download & Complete

Download the PDF application form. Please fill it out in clear Block Letters and sign the Statement of Faith.

Download PDF Form
03

Gather Attachments

Scan your UCE/UACE passlips, a copy of your National ID, and a signed recommendation letter from your local Pastor.

04

Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable fee via Mobile Money or Bank Deposit. Attach a screenshot or scan of your receipt to your application email.

05

Submit via Email

Send all documents to admissions@ubseminary.ac.ug.
Use the subject line: Application: [Your Name] - [Program]

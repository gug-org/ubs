Welcome

Message from the Principal

"Welcome to Uganda Baptist Seminary. For decades, our singular mission has been to train men and women who are deeply rooted in the Scriptures, passionately devoted to Jesus Christ, and thoroughly equipped to serve His Church.

Whether you are called to pastoral ministry, cross-cultural missions, or theological scholarship, you will find a community here that challenges you academically and nurtures you spiritually. I invite you to explore our campus, engage with our faculty, and partner with us in what God is doing in East Africa."

Our Legacy

A Heritage of Faith

Located in the heart of Jinja, near the source of the Nile, Uganda Baptist Seminary has stood as a beacon of biblical truth since its founding. We are committed to the inerrancy of Scripture and the transformative power of the Gospel.

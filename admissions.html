Application Guide

Welcome to the Admissions Office. We are pleased to assist you in your journey toward theological training. Follow the steps below to submit your application via email.

01

Confirm Eligibility ➔

Ensure you meet the NCHE minimum requirements for your desired program:

  • Certificate: UCE (O-Level) with 3 passes.
  • Diploma: UACE (A-Level) with 1 Principal Pass & 2 Subsidiaries.
  • Bachelor's: UACE (A-Level) with 2 Principal Passes.
02

Download & Complete ➔

Download the application form. Please fill it out in clear Block Letters and sign the Statement of Faith section.

Download PDF Form
03

Gather Attachments ➔

Scan your UCE/UACE passlips, a copy of your National ID, and a signed recommendation letter from your local Pastor or Bishop.

04

Application Fee ➔

Pay the non-refundable fee via Mobile Money or Bank Deposit. Attach a screenshot or scan of your receipt to your email.

05

Submit via Email ➔

Send all documents to info@ugandabaptistseminary.org
Subject: Application: [Your Name] - [Program]

Compose Email Now

Questions? Contact the Registrar's Office at +256 771 136 567 or visit our Jinja Campus.
